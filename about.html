About Michael Benson ColorCast

Michael Benson ColorCast is a vibrant weather application that provides real-time weather updates and stunning visuals corresponding to your searched locations.

Utilizing the Open-Meteo and Unsplash APIs, ColorCast delivers accurate weather data complemented by beautiful imagery.

Our mission is to make weather forecasting not only accurate but also visually engaging for users worldwide.
